About

Institute will register on website and feed the data of their students and faculties.

Institute will send link of google form/meet via any social authentication.

Student will open the link, Our Extension will open simultaneously.

Student have to enter Aadhar no.

Extention will check students detail with our database and then with UIDAI database.

If verified SMS/Mail will be sent on students mobile no. or email through API service.

After verfication student will have access of further process.

If not verified in any of the above step Extension will be redirected on Aadhar authentication page.
